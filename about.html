About NEU'MOR

Our Story

From a Home Workshop to a Brand

NEU'MOR is an Indonesian women's fashion brand founded by our family. What started as a small workshop in our home has grown into a label worn across the archipelago.

We believe in dressing women in garments that honour tradition while embracing the modern world. Every piece is cut, sewn, and finished with care.

What We Stand For

Craft, Family, Heritage

  • Craftsmanship

    Every garment is cut and sewn with precision and care.

  • Family

    We are a family business — when you wear NEU'MOR, you wear something made with love.

  • Heritage

    Our designs are rooted in Indonesian tradition, reimagined for the modern woman.

Growing Together

Every Order, With the Same Love

As word spread, so did our collection. We expanded from simple blouses to full lines of tops and bottoms.

Today, we offer free shipping across Java and accept corporate uniform orders — all with the same care that started it all.
